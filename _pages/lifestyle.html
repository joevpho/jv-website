Since I collaborate a lot with other members of the "lifestyle" and, a lot of same values around privacy and respect in these communities are also very important for me and my work. I thought I'd talk a bit more about it and what it means...

Adult Lifestyle... What do you mean?

One quick definition that I read somewhere summarizes Adult Lifestyle as a term used to convey the adventurous practices of couples and singles that enjoy recreational sex with other consenting adults, otherwise known as swingers.

I really like to think it as adults free to live their sexuality free of judgement or taboos, enabled to explore and be explored, respecting each other individuality, space, comfort zones and privacy.

What about it and photography?

In a similar way to what mentioned about intimate photography, security, trust and respect are extremely important in the lifestyle community. Photographing lifestyle events or parties, like erotic or intimate photography, can produce sexually explicit content and everyone taking part of the session needs the peace of mind that their security and privacy will be violated. My policy is to always have an agreement with anyone involved defining how and what images can be used and/or shared, for what purposes and under what conditions. It can go from "hey, we are all exhibitionists and are happy to share it to the world" to "this is a private event and all of the images produced are private, not to share" to somewhere in between where only the non recognizable pictures are to share. The point is that everything is decided and agreed upon together. My collaborators/partners/clients privacy is my highest priority.

In a similar way to my erotic sessions, these sessions often produce sexually explicit images and for this reason, the majority of the images are kept private for private clients or shared only in a private way (on OnlyFans, for instance) upon everyone's consent. Most will never be shared on social platforms.
